Learning to List

There are two kinds of lists you can add to your website using HTML. The first choice is an orderd list, and the second choice is an unorderd list. Ordered lists are numbered, whilst unorderd lists show up on your website in bulleted form.

Lists are helpful for:
  • Ranking content
  • In step-by-step tutorials
  • Listing things in chronological order
They are also helpful when
  1. You have many points for the same overall topic
  2. Used in slideshows to make reading and conceptual understanding easier
  3. Lists make main ideas or topics clear to the reader

Ordered Lists

    <ol>  (numbered)
      <li>A Day</i>
      <li>Spanish</li>
   </ol>

Unorderd Lists

    <ul>    (bulleted)
      <li> A Day</i>
      <li>Spanish</li>
    </ul>
